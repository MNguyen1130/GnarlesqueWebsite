Who am I?
Welcome, awesome individuals reading this thing! Nice to meet you!

▪I’m Gnarlesque (AKA Gnarly or Gnar)—an artsy dork who’s doing their best to overcome anxiety. I try to be friendly and fun if I ever make conversation though!

✫A few rad things I like are: lighthearted humor, pixel art(!!!), happy fluffy animals, illustration/animation (mainly anime and cartoons), bright colors, quirky/epic fantasy video games! Chiptune, techno, electronic, and j-pop are some music genres I significantly love!

♦I’m agnostic, panromantic, as well as agender (‘they/them/their’ pronouns preferred!), and androgynous (a work-in-progress but I will get there someway).

▪Browse here for an idea on my personality but you can talk to me too or just explore my blog! I reblog a lotta content I may not be familiar with just because I happen to like what I see so that’s important to know!

With that said, I hope we can get along! *⌒∇⌒*

Process:
  • I will sketch a drawing for you to approve. You can ask for changes at this time, but I’ll only do this once. Any extras will cost more! Make it count~
  • Most commissions are done within first week or two they were accepted/paid for, but I recommend giving me up to a month to account for any other commissions/life obligations.
  • I may post works-in-progress/livestream the commission, so let me know if you wouldn’t like that.
How Payment Works:
  • I don’t start a commission until I have full or at least half payment! I take payments via Paypal. I also send invoices to my dear commissioners using the email provided in the form so all you need to do is confirm payment!
  • There will be NO refunds once a payment is made!
Other Info:
  • A lot of my art, regardless of style, tends to have a ‘cute’ atmosphere!
  • I will have my watermark/signature on the image. Please don’t remove it–it’s to prevent false ownership claims.
  • I typically post finished commissions on my social media sites and may use it as a future examples or possible merchandise. Please let me know if you want to keep it private or have it as a surprise!
